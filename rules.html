Rules Of The Game

Points-Rule
Winning Points

Tournament Team Winner: 10 points.
Tournament Top Scorer: 10 points.

Correct WLD

Group Staged: 3 points.
Round of 16: 5 points.
QuarterFinals: 6 points.
SemiFinals: 7 points.
3/4 place: 8 points.
Final: 8 points.

Exact Score

For all tournament stages: 1 point.

Bonus Points

Exact Score in a game with 4+ goals: 1 point.
Your chosen Top scorer player scores a goal: 1 point.
For each team correct position in the group standing: 1 point.

Correct Playoff Winner
 Round of 16: 3 points.
 QuarterFinals: 4 points.
 SemiFinals: 5 points.
 3/4 place: 6 points.
 Final: 6 points.

How To Play

When you register into the game, you will need to choose a winning team that you think will win the tournament and choose the Top scorer Player.
But don't worry, you can still change your choices as long as the tournament hasn't stated.
Additionally, you will need to choose the position of each team in each group based on how you think they will rank at the end of the group stage.
For each match, guess the score of the match. you will get points on exact score and on correct WLD (win,lose,draw). for knockout stages, you will get points if you guessed the correct playoff winner team as well.

Term Of Use

The match's final score is after 90 min, not including extra time and penalties.
The winning team and the Top scorer player can be changed until the kickoff of the first match of the tournament (AKA until June 16, 22:00).
You can enter or change the match's score until the start of that same match.
For group standing, you can enter and changed the team's position until the kickoff of the first match of the tournament.
A user that will enter after the tournament has started, won't get points for the missing matches.
